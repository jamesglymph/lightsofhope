Design Challenge

Anderson Lights of Hope

What's the problem you're trying to solve?

Providing affordable events for the betterment of the local community while simultaneously giving back to it.

Design Question

How does the ALOH website communicate its goal?

Ultimate Impact

Increase awareness of ALOH and what exactly their goals are.

Possible Solutions

  • More details on events (as opposed to completely separate websites)
  • A clear schedule/calendar of when those events occur
  • A more clear-cut mission statement

Context & Constraints

  • Local organization, Anderson county
  • Goals of ALOH are spread out across different sites and lack a clear focus

New Question

How can the ALOH website be redesigned to better focus the goals and purpose of the events and promote said events?
