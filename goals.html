Audience & Goals

Anderson Lights of Hope

Determining the Audience

The audience of ALOH was tricky to determine, as the events they host seem to be meant for literally anyone. However, an interview with ALOH’s Ben Philips gave some insight as to how they target their audience. The audience of ALOH consists of people in the Anderson community, but they’re actually a minority of the audience, oddly enough. ALOH actually does a lot of advertising outside the Anderson area (about a 50-mile radius) in order to net more people who may have not heard of their events. Within these areas they primarily target families, as those are the people who come to events like the light show.

I thought about volunteers and donors as obvious outlets of help for ALOH, as a lot of what they do relies on outside help. These sources of help could range from college-aged students, to other non-profits in cahoots with ALOH. In the interview, Ben Philips briefly mentioned how the city council has been an obstacle for some of their events in the past, so I took note of them as a possible skeptic.

Overarching Goals

Client

  • To raise money at their events and (after paying the event bills) donate any excess funds to other charities and non-profits.
  • Help children and adults with disabilities
  • Take care of those who can’t take care of themselves
  • Target people outside the Anderson circle (50-mile radius)

Audience

  • When/what/where events are happening
  • Opportunities to volunteer at events
  • Donating to the cause

Website

  • Obvious prompts for donating and volunteering
  • Calendar offering details on events
  • Reaching local communities around the Anderson area

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Parents with an average income Attend the events for fun with the family Calendar and details of events
Students Volunteer, Attend the events Fill out volunteer forms, Calendar and details of events
Kids Attend the events for fun with the family Calendar and details of events
Sponsors Donate, Raise awareness of ALOH and what they do Donation prompts and forms, Clear mission and premise of ALOH
School extracurricular teams Volunteer, Donate, Raise awareness of ALOH Fill out volunteer forms, Donation prompts and forms, Clear mission and premise of ALOH
Church teams Volunteer, Donate Fill out volunteer forms, Donation prompts and forms

Conclusion

I would say the overall goal of the ALOH website is simply to be more informative. Whether it’s details on events, their mission statement, or simply making a volunteer form, the website will not be able to serve its purpose effectively without a clear hierarchy and call to action.
